###### Red Hat OpenShift AI is a platform for managing the lifecycle of predictive and generative AI (gen AI) models, at scale, across hybrid cloud environments. Built using open source technologies, OpenShift AI provides trusted, operationally consistent capabilities for teams to experiment, serve models, and deliver innovative applications. OpenShift AI enables data acquisition and preparation, model training and fine-tuning, model serving and model monitoring, and hardware acceleration.
